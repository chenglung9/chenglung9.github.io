About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Department of Industrial Engineering and Management Systems at University of Central Florida. I am advised by Dr.Vladimir Boginski in the Applied Operations Research Lab. I receive a master degree in operations research from the Ohio State University, a master degree in industrial engineering from Arizona State University and a bachelor degree in business administration from Soochow University, Taiwan. I am interested in mixed-integer programming, network optimization, optimization under uncertainty and their applications.
